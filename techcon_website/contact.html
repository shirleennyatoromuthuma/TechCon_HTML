History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the years, it has evolved into a global stage for breakthrough technologies, welcoming thousands of attendees annually. From its humble beginnings focused on open-source tools, TechCon has expanded to include innovations in AI, cybersecurity, robotics, and more.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire, connect, and empower the global tech community. We believe in fostering innovation through collaboration, promoting ethical technology, and creating a platform where voices from all backgrounds can shape the digital future. Whether you're a student, startup founder, or seasoned professional, TechCon is your launchpad for making a meaningful impact in tech
